Robotics Team 2017

Programmer
Project Overview
Apart of the For Inspiration and Recognition Of Science and Technology (FIRST) 2017-2018 season. In FIRST teams design, program, and build a robot starting with common set of rules to play in a themed head-to-head challenge. Teams also build a brand, develop community partnerships for support, and work to promote STEM in their local community.
My Contributions
On my first year on the team, I collaborated with the programming team in order to implement new algorithms for the autonomous period of the robot. I also began research on computer vision, and the different ways to implement it in competition.
This was my first year on the robotics team, and with this experience I was able to learn more about the fields of engineering, circuit design, programming, CAD, and develop soft skills such as giving a presentation our development and the final product in front of technical and non-technical people, and learning how to network with others. I also gained experience in working on a team, conflict resolution, and project management.
I programmed the grabber mechanism and the rotating arm. I collaborated with the programming team on creating the linear slide algorithm to extend with a certain power depending on how the controller was pressed.
