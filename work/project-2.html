Cardboard Chair

Engineering
Project Overview
In this project, we evaluated what data is necessary to make cogent design decisions, specifically for designing a chair. We composed sketches of possible cardboard chair designs, showing relevant details and dimensions, evaluated sketches for design considerations, and selected a final design for scale modeling and further evaluation. We constructed a scale model and executed an evaluation plan that Â prescribed to evaluate the preliminary design. Then we created a professional drawing of our final design, and from our testing of the scale model, and composed a build plan to construct our full-scale chair. We constructed our full-scale chair out of cardboard and demonstrated it in class when an instructor sat in it. We concluded by presenting our process, product, and results.
My Contributions
I collaborated on the design of the chair. I also composed the sketches, evaluated the details and dimensions, and also built the final product.
In this project I was able to use what I learned in academics and apply in practice. I researched the ergonomics of a chair for the average adult male and female. Once we knew our constraints, we began coming up with various designs and then we started to sketch and dimension those designs. Once we were finished with those calculations, we scaled down our design in order to make a prototype to test if our chair was viable. After putting our prototype through various tests, we made improvements to its design and began building the final product.
