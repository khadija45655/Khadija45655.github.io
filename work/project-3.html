Straw Tower

Engineering
Project Overview
Our task was to construct a tall straw tower out of 30 plastic straws and tape that could withstand the weight of 3 large textbooks.
My Contributions
I collaborated with my team on the design and building of the tower.
In order to construct our tower to be able to handle the weight of the textbooks, we wanted the tower to have multiple points of contact to the ground, so that the pressure from the books became evenly distributed closer to the base of the tower. We decided on a pentagon as the base, while using the corners and the middle to disperse the load evenly.
